About Kingsukh Guest House

Nestled in the serene embrace of nature, Kingsukh Guest House offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. We are ideally located, providing breathtaking views and easy access to some of the most captivating natural landmarks in the region.

Surrounded by the majestic Biharinath Hill, the picturesque Baranti Hill, the historic Susunia Hill, and the charming Joychandi Hill, our guest house is a gateway to exploration and tranquility. Discover the ancient ruins of Garhpanchkot, or unwind by the tranquil waters of Baranti Dam, Maithon Dam, and the captivating Panchat Dam.

"A truly peaceful experience!"
